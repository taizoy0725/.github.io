Rolled Paper Calculator - 巻き紙電卓 -

Features of this App

Rolled Paper Calculator has award for 2010 the best iPhone App in Japan (AppBank)
Ver7.0 has decimal calc engine.
Roll Paper Calculator records all input and result history, then you can confirm your process of calculation
This App outputs the history of calculation like print the calc record to the paper. And you can see all the calculation process on the paper using your finger.
Usage: calculation while shopping, calculation house hold budget, etc.
