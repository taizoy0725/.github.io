Makiden Lite

An Innovative Calculator with History and Simplicity

Makiden Lite is a revolutionary calculator app that combines ease of use with powerful functionality. It maintains the familiar operations of a traditional calculator while introducing a unique paper roll display for calculation history.

Recognized as one of the Best iPhone Apps of 2010 by AppBank, it has gained widespread user appreciation. Since version 7.0, it has incorporated a decimal arithmetic engine, enhancing calculation accuracy. Make your daily calculations easier and more precise.

Use cases:
Shopping expense calculations, household budget verifications, business expense calculations, educational math tool, and more.

Key Features

1. Paper Roll Display

View your calculation process as if on a paper roll, allowing you to check your work at any time. Easily review past calculations by scrolling.

2. Standard/Advanced Keyboard Switch

New feature: Choose between Standard and Advanced keyboard modes to suit your preferences and calculation needs.

  • Standard: Offers simple, easy-to-use basic calculator functions
  • Advanced: Provides an extended keyboard with more advanced features

3. Flexible Decimal Point Modes

Three decimal point modes available:

  • F (Free): No limit on decimal places
  • 0 (Zero): Integer-only display
  • 2 (Euro mode): Display up to 2 decimal places

Easily switch using the "dp/VAT" button (Advanced mode).

4. VAT Calculation Feature

In Advanced mode, long-press the "dp/VAT" button to set the VAT rate. Once set, simply activate +VAT mode with the "dp/VAT" button to automatically include tax in your calculations.

5. Scanning Function

Use the "SCAN" button in Advanced mode to scan numbers from receipts or price lists for automatic input. Particularly useful for accurately entering large numbers, such as for tax returns.

6. Advanced Calculation Features

  • Square Root: Easily calculate square roots with the "√" button.
  • Sign Toggle: Switch between positive and negative numbers with the "±↻" button.
  • Backspace Function: Delete the last digit in case of input errors (Advanced mode).
  • Restore Function: Recover deleted digits (Advanced mode).
  • C/AC Function: Press "C/AC" to reset the current input or calculation result. Long-press to clear all content from the paper roll.

7. Copy Function

In Advanced mode, use the "copy" button to copy the calculation history displayed on the paper roll to your clipboard.

8. Total Calculation Function

The "=/Σ" button displays normal calculation results. In Advanced mode, long-press to calculate the sum of all results shown on the paper roll.

9. User-Friendly Interface

  • Intuitive operation
  • Clear display
  • Dark mode support
  • Easy switching between Standard and Advanced modes
