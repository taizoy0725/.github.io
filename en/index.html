Rolled Paper Calculator

App Features

The Rolled Paper Calculator, selected as one of the Best iPhone Apps of 2010 by AppBank, is an innovative calculator app. Version 7.0 introduces a decimal calculation engine, significantly improving calculation accuracy.

This app records calculation history as if outputting results on paper. You can easily review all calculation processes by scrolling through the results with your finger.

Use cases: Shopping expense calculations, household budget verification, expense calculations, educational calculation tool, and more.
